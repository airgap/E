# A real IDE. On your phone.

Not a viewer, not a companion app — the whole of E, responsive. The editor, agent chat, terminal, and git all reflow to a single column. No other IDE does this.

- **Responsive by default** — the same UI scales from a multi-pane desktop split to a one-column phone view. No separate app.
- **Reach your machine** — open E from your phone over Tailscale or an SSH tunnel, authenticated every time.
- **Touch-first** — swipe between panes, tap to run, long-press for actions.

> This page is E's actual UI. Shrink the window (or open it on your phone) and it folds into the mobile layout — live.
